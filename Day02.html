Spacekit

Scape’s SDK, called ‘ScapeKit’, recognizes the precise location of mobile devices outdoors, allowing AR content to be placed in exact geographic locations.

Introducing ScapeKit

Designed for large-scale AR

  • Unlike other SDKs for AR, ScapeKit is the only solution that provides outdoor location recognition at a city-scale, so that you can accurately lock content to the world, across an entire city.
  • Like a camera-enabled GPS and compass, ScapeKit provides hyper-accurate location and orientation in geographical coordinates.
  • ScapeKit supports ARKit and ARCore compatible devices, providing precise geo-location for both Android and iOS.
